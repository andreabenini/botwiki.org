Cheap Bots, Done Quick! is an [open source](https://github.com/v21?utf8=%E2%9C%93&tab=repositories&q=traceryhosting&type=&language=) online tool created by George Buckenham that "will help you make a Twitterbot" using Tracery, a tool for writing generative grammars developed by Kate Compton.

Also check out the CBDQ! tutorials and an interview with George published in our Bot! zine.
